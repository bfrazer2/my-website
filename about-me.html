About Me

Defining oneself is impossible without being either verbose or reductive, and here I'll err towards the latter.

I am driven by the desires to experience and to improve. I am a thrill seeker-I enjoy apline sports, roller coasters and rock climbing. Staying active is important to me so I also play competitive sports like basketball and squash, as they marry my desire for adrenaline and my internal competitiveness well.

These defining drives have also led me towards self-teaching programming, pushing myself creatively through poetry, music, and acting, and fostering a regular gym habit to stay healthy and strong.

In that we are all much more than a collection of interests this list is reductive to the point of comedy, but it outlines the shape of my passion, which tend to be the boundaries we color most deeply.
